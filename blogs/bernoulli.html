Understanding the Bernoulli Equation

The Bernoulli Equationis one of the most important relationships in fluid mechanics. It connects pressure, velocity,and elevation of a flowing fluid and helps engineers analyze how fluids behave in pipes, pumps, tanks, reactors, and process equipment.

What is Bernoulli Equation

For steady, incompressible, and frictionless flow, the Bernoulli equation along a streamline is:

Mathematical Formula

\[ P + \frac{1}{2}\rho v^2 + \rho g h = \text{constant} \]
